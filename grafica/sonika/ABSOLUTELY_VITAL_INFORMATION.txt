Sonika is a sleek sans-serif typeface. 
With space age curves and sharp corners, it is a type to make your project forward-looking. 

Sonika comes as a family of five distinct weights:
Sonika Thin, Sonika Light, Sonika Regular, Sonika Bold and Sonika Black
In addition to that there are italics, adding up to a total of ten high-quality fonts. 

This font also supports a majority of European and Latin-based languages.
